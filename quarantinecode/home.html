Fun During Social Distancing

We are a group of students at SFSU that have taken into hands to create an organized and categorized website from an existing google doc full of quarantine games. Given that we are undergoing a global pandemic and are forced to stay home, it can be really hard to find ways to connect with your friends and family. For this reason, we are trying to provide an environment where people can connect and have fun while social distancing.

Have fun, and stay safe!
